Yes, you can fully customize your dashboards to display the most important metrics. Tailor reports to track key performance indicators and focus on what drives your business forward. Get insights that matter most to you.

How does the task management feature help me delegate tasks to my team and track progress?

Easily delegate tasks and monitor progress in real-time. Keep everyone on track with clear assignments and deadlines. Organize, prioritize, and ensure your team stays aligned with your goals.

Can I collaborate with my team in real-time using all tools?

Yes, collaborate in real-time with messaging and video tools. Share files, schedule meetings, and stay connected seamlessly. Ensure your team is always on the same page.

Is the app available on all devices?

Yes, access "Growth" on any device—desktop, tablet, or smartphone. Stay connected whether you're in the office or on the go. Full functionality, wherever you are.

How does the app help me stay compliant when working with freelancers and contractors?

Manage compliance effortlessly. Store contracts, track work hours, and ensure all legal requirements are met. Keep everything transparent and compliant, reducing risk and ensuring smooth operations.

Sign up with your email, create a password, and you're ready to go. Follow the setup wizard to customize your account and start organizing your business.

Can I import my existing data?

Yes, easily import your existing contacts, tasks, and data. Get started quickly by bringing in your current information. Seamlessly transition into using "Growth."

How do I invite my team to join?

Invite your team members by sending them an email invite from the platform. They can join your workspace and start collaborating immediately.

Connect your favorite apps with our integration options. Streamline your workflow by linking the tools you already use.
